We understand the geology of DFW area’s clay soil and the board range of problems it can cause. Our experts have the skills, knowledge and resources required to address your foundation problems. Our Secure-Lock foundation repair procedure means your foundation is good for forever. One time for you, The whole life for us! Foundation repair with lifetime transferable warranty.


Normal procedure starts with your call to schedule no obligation free foundation inspection. We visit your home and evaluate your foundation. We provide you tentative plan for foundation repair if your foundation needs repair. Then we hire 3rd party structural engineer who inspects your foundation and provides final pier plan for foundation repair. We pull the permit from your city and implement the foundation repairs as per the plan provided by structural engineer. Engineer inspects our work and provides the “Engineer Report” with final readings for your foundation. We issue lifetime transferable warranty certificate after you make your final payment. Now your foundation is good for FOREVER!!
