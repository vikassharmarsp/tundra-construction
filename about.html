Company Profile

Tundra Construction is one stop shop for all of your Home Improvement needs. Be it Roofing, Foundation Repair, Kitchen-Bathroom remodeling, adding new room or bathroom to your sweet home. Company owner TJ Singh is a US Army Veteran and is committed to providing not only stunning results, but also ensuring that the entire process is a pleasant experience for the homeowners.

TJ has 13 years of experience in the home improvement industry. He brings a truly professional and trusted team of trade professionals who meet his high standards of excellence. TJ’s personal attention to the details and professionalism during consultation, planning and execution of the project will leave you eager to refer Tundra Construction to homeowners in your social networks !

For homeowners peace of mind, we share references of previous customers and certificates of Bond and liability Insurance.
